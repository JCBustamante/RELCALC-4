Consider the vectors \((1,2,-1)\text{,}\) and \((0,5,-2)\) and call \(S\) the span of the two vectors. Let us find an orthogonal basis of \(S\text{:}\)
\begin{align*} \vec{w}_1 \amp = (1,2,-1) ,\\ \vec{w}_2 \amp = (0,5,-2) - \operatorname{proj}_{(1,2,-1)}\bigl((0,2,-2)\bigr)\\ \amp = (0,1,-1) - \frac{\langle (0,5,-2), (1,2,-1) \rangle}{ \langle (1,2,-1), (1,2,-1) \rangle } (1,2,-1) = (0,5,-2) - 2 (1,2,-1) = (-2,1,0) \text{.} \end{align*}
