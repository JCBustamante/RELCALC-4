One application of matrices is to solve systems of linear equations 1 . Consider the following system of linear equations
\begin{equation} \begin{aligned} 2 x_1 + 2 x_2 + 2 x_3 \amp = 2 , \\ x_1 + x_2 + 3 x_3 \amp = 5 , \\ x_1 + 4 x_2 + x_3 \amp = 10 . \end{aligned}\tag{6.3.1} \end{equation}
