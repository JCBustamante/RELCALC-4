Let us double check that the projection is orthogonal. That is \(\vec{w}-\operatorname{proj}_{\vec{v}}(\vec{w})\) ought to be orthogonal to \(\vec{v}\text{,}\) see the right angle in FigureÂ 6.5.3. That is,
\begin{equation*} (3,2,1) - \operatorname{proj}_{(1,2,3)} \bigl( (3,2,1) \bigr) = \left(3-\frac{5}{7},2-\frac{10}{7},1-\frac{15}{7}\right) = \left(\frac{16}{7},\frac{4}{7},\frac{-8}{7}\right) \end{equation*}
ought to be orthogonal to \((1,2,3)\text{.}\) We compute the inner product and we had better get zero:
\begin{equation*} \left\langle \left(\frac{16}{7},\frac{4}{7},\frac{-8}{7}\right) , (1,2,3) \right\rangle = \frac{16}{7} \cdot 1 + \frac{4}{7} \cdot 2 -\frac{8}{7} \cdot 3 = 0\text{.} \end{equation*}
