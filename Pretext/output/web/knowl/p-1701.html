Let us return to the equations themselves and see how we can manipulate them. There are a few operations we can perform on the equations that do not change the solution. First, perhaps an operation that may seem stupid, we can swap two equations in (6.3.1):
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} x_1 + x_2 + 3 x_3 \amp = 5 , \\ 2 x_1 + 2 x_2 + 2 x_3 \amp = 2 , \\ x_1 + 4 x_2 + x_3 \amp = 10 . \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
