For example, the vectors \(\left[ \begin{matrix}1 \\ 2 \end{matrix} \right]\) and \(\left[ \begin{matrix}0 \\ 1 \end{matrix} \right]\) are linearly independent. Let's try:
\begin{equation*} \alpha_1 \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} + \alpha_2 \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \alpha_1 \\ 2 \alpha_1 + \alpha_2 \end{bmatrix} = \vec{0} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
