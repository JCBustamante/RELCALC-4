Example 6.5.2.

Let us compute the angle between the vectors \((3,0)\) and \((1,1)\) in the plane. Compute
\begin{equation*} \cos \theta = \frac{\bigl\langle (3,0) , (1,1) \bigr\rangle}{\lVert(3,0)\rVert \lVert(1,1)\rVert} = \frac{3 + 0}{3 \sqrt{2}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Therefore \(\theta = \nicefrac{\pi}{4}\text{.}\)
