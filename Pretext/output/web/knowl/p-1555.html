To represent the direction of a vector \(\vec{x}\text{,}\) we need to find the unit vector in the same direction. To do so, we simply rescale \(\vec{x}\) by the reciprocal of the magnitude, that is \(\frac{1}{\lVert \vec{x} \rVert} \vec{x}\text{,}\) or more concisely \(\frac{\vec{x}}{\lVert \vec{x} \rVert}\text{.}\)
