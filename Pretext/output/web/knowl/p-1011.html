Si l'on calcule les valeurs propres de la matrice \(P = \left[ \begin{matrix}1 \amp 1 \\ -1 \amp 1 \end{matrix} \right]\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} \det(P - \lambda I) = \det\left( \begin{bmatrix} 1-\lambda \amp 1 \\ -1 \amp 1-\lambda \end{bmatrix} \right) = {(1-\lambda)}^2 + 1 = \lambda^2 - 2 \lambda + 2 = 0\text{.} \end{equation*}
