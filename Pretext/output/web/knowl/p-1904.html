Suppose we wish to project the vector \((1,2,3)\) onto the vector \((3,2,1)\text{.}\) Compute
\begin{equation*} \begin{split} \operatorname{proj}_{(1,2,3)} \bigl( (3,2,1) \bigr) = \frac{\langle (3,2,1) , (1,2,3) \rangle}{\langle (1,2,3) , (1,2,3) \rangle} (1,2,3) \amp = \frac{3 \cdot 1 + 2 \cdot 2 + 1 \cdot 3}{ 1 \cdot 1 + 2 \cdot 2 + 3 \cdot 3} (1,2,3) \\ \amp = \frac{10}{14} (1,2,3) = \left(\frac{5}{7},\frac{10}{7},\frac{15}{7}\right) . \end{split} \end{equation*}
